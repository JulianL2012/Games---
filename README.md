# 🎮 Games Hub — 32 Browser Games

> **All games are coded in pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no downloads, no plugins, no accounts.  
> Just open a file in any browser and play instantly!**

---

## 📖 About This Repository

This is a massive collection of **32 browser-based games**, all built from scratch using pure **HTML5 Canvas and Vanilla JavaScript**.  
Every game is a single self-contained `.html` file — open it and play. No build tools, no dependencies, no internet required after downloading.

The games range from timeless **classics** (Snake, Tetris, Pac-Man, Chess, Solitaire) to **classroom favorites** (Cookie Clicker, Slope, Wordle, Geometry Dash, Dino Jump), **action/shooting** games (Space Invaders, Asteroids, Zombie Shooter), and even a **Five Nights at Freddy's-inspired horror game** (Night Watch).

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

### 🌐 Instant Play (Any Computer)
1. Open the repository on GitHub.
2. Click on any `.html` file (e.g. `games/snake.html`).
3. Click **"Raw"** to open the raw file, or download it.
4. Open it in **Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari**.
5. Play!

### 💻 Play Everything at Once — Local Hub
1. **Download the entire repository** as a ZIP:  
   - Click the green **Code** button → **Download ZIP**
2. Extract the ZIP anywhere on your computer.
3. Open `index.html` in your browser.
4. This loads the **Game Hub** — a visual menu of all 32 games.
5. Click any game card to open and play!

### 📱 Chromebook Instructions
1. Download the ZIP (see above).
2. Open the **Files** app and find your Downloads folder.
3. Extract the ZIP (right-click → Extract All).
4. Open `index.html` — it will launch in Chrome automatically.
5. Or navigate to `chrome://newtab` and drag `index.html` in.

### 📱 Mobile / Tablet
All games support **touch and swipe** controls where relevant.  
Open `index.html` on your phone browser and play — no app required.

## 🎯 Featured Highlights

### 🌙 Night Watch (FNAF-Inspired Horror)
The most complex game in the collection. You play as a night security guard working a 12 AM to 6 AM shift. Animatronic characters roam the building and you must:
- **Check security cameras** to track their locations
- **Close the doors** to block their entry to your office
- **Manage your power** — everything drains it and when power hits zero the lights go out
- Survive all **6 in-game hours** to beat the night

Difficulty increases each night. Inspired by Five Nights at Freddy's.

### 👻 Pac-Man
Full Pac-Man implementation with ghost AI that switches between chase and scatter behavior, power pellets that make ghosts vulnerable, level progression, and animated ghosts and Pac-Man character.

### ♟️ Chess
Fully legal-move-validated chess with check/checkmate/stalemate detection, move highlighting, captured piece display, undo, and a minimax-based AI opponent option.

### 🂡 Klondike Solitaire
Complete Solitaire with drag-based card movement, proper stacking rules, ace foundation building, undo history, and hint mode.

### 🚀 Asteroids
Full physics-based Asteroids with asteroid splitting on destruction, ship thrust and rotation, bullet trajectory with ship velocity, a rechargeable shield, and particle effects.
